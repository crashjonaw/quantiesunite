About QuantiesUnite

Two Singaporeans. One mission. Give every learner the power to understand mathematics from the ground up — and the confidence to reach beyond what school alone can offer.

Our Vision

We envision a Singapore where every student — regardless of school, background, or starting point — has access to a complete mathematical education that takes them from counting objects on a table to understanding how the AI systems shaping their world actually work.

We believe that in an age where AI can write code, generate art, and pass exams, the most valuable skill is not memorization — it is understanding. The ability to think from first principles, to ask "why does this work?", and to build new knowledge from the foundations up.

That is what QuantiesUnite is for.

From Kindergarten to Deep Learning. From first principles. For every learner.

What We Believe

🌱

First Principles, Always

We never ask you to memorize a formula without showing you where it comes from. Every concept is derived, motivated, and connected. When you understand why, you never forget how.

🔗

Everything is Connected

Mathematics is not a collection of isolated topics. Fractions lead to ratio, ratio leads to proportion, proportion leads to probability, probability leads to machine learning. We make those connections visible.

🚀

No Ceiling on Learning

School syllabuses end, but curiosity doesn't. A Primary 6 student who masters PSLE math should be able to keep going — into algebra, calculus, and beyond — without waiting for permission.

🇸🇬

Proudly Singaporean

We align to the MOE curriculum because we believe in it. Our question styles, difficulty levels, and topic progression reflect the Singapore approach — disciplined, structured, and effective.

Built for Singapore. Built for the Future.

Singapore's education system is world-class. Our students consistently rank among the best in international mathematics assessments. We are proud of that legacy — and we want to build on it.

But the world is changing fast. Artificial intelligence is no longer a distant concept — it is here, transforming every industry, every career, every way we learn and work. The students sitting in classrooms today will graduate into a world where understanding AI isn't optional. It's foundational.

Yet our curriculum stops at A-Levels. The bridge from school mathematics to the mathematics of AI — linear algebra, probability, neural networks — is one that most students have to cross alone, if they cross it at all.

QuantiesUnite closes that gap.

We take the best of Singapore's structured, rigorous approach to mathematics education and extend it — from Kindergarten all the way to Deep Learning. Every topic is aligned to the MOE syllabus where it applies, and every advanced topic is built on the foundations that came before it.

Our Story

We grew up in Singapore's education system. We sat for the same national exams, attended the same tuition centres, and memorized the same formulas. And like so many students here, we did well — but we didn't always understand.

It wasn't until later in our careers — one of us deep in quantitative finance and machine learning, the other in food science and nutrition research — that we realized how much of mathematics we had to re-learn from scratch. Not because the content had changed, but because we had never truly learned why things worked. We'd memorized procedures without building intuition.

That realization became the seed of QuantiesUnite.

We asked ourselves: What if a student could learn mathematics the way we wish we had — starting from first principles, with every concept connected to the one before it, all the way from counting to neural networks?

And so we built it.
